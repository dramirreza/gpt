1. Why IMAT 2025 Matters

The International Medical Admissions Test is the official entrance exam for English-taught medical and dental programs at Italian public universities. More than 10,000 applicants compete for fewer than 1,200 seats, so preparation and timing are essential.

2. IMAT 2025: What Has Changed

4. Registration Process

EU & EU-Equivalent

  1. Register on universitaly.it
  2. Choose universities (preference order matters)
  3. Pay the fee and confirm test centre

Non-EU (Residing Abroad)

  1. Pre-enrol on Universitaly by 31 July 2025
  2. Upload passport, diploma, and Declaration of Value or CIMEA verification
  3. Register for IMAT and choose an international test centre

7. Cut-Off Scores 2024

Most competitive: Bologna (65.6 EU / 74.5 non-EU). Close second: Milan (69.2 EU / 75.3 non-EU). Most accessible: Cagliari (56.9 EU / 56.5 non-EU).

8. Test Centre Locations

Take the IMAT in Italy at your first-choice university or at international centres worldwide: London, Paris, Dublin, Madrid, Berlin, New York, Toronto, Dubai, New Delhi, Hong Kong, Beijing, Johannesburg and more.

9. Frequently Asked Questions
  • Is the IMAT cancelled in 2025? → No. The reform excludes English-language degrees.
  • Can I take IMAT online? → No, it is in-person only.
  • What score is competitive? → Aim for ≥65 (EU) or ≥70 (non-EU).
  • Can I change university preferences after registration? → No, preferences are locked.
