TL;DR for the Frantic Scroller

  • Exam date: Wednesday 17 September 2025, same global start-time system as always.
  • Registration window opens mid-summer with a 2-3 week deadline set in the MUR decree.
  • No format shake-up: 60 Q / 100 min / paper-based (4 GK, 5 Logic, 23 Bio, 15 Chem, 13 Phys+Math).
  • Universities: 16 public med schools + 2 dentistry programmes (Cagliari newest).
  • Pre-enrolment for non-EU closes 31 July 2025 (upload diploma + DoV/CIMEA).
  • Toughest 2024 cut-offs: Bologna 74.5 (non-EU) & Milan Statale 69.2 (EU); easiest Cagliari 56.5/56.9.
  • Italian open-semester reform excludes English courses, so IMAT stays for 2025.
